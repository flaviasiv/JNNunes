About us

Back in 2019, our family decided to start a new chapter, leaving Brazil for bigger things in Columbus, Ohio. We jumped into cleaning homes, apartments, and work spots.

Our motto? Responsibility, realness, and full-on dedication. Every house we clean reflects our commitment, our journey, and our wish to make every client feel like their place gets the love our own home does.

At J&N Nunes Cleaning Services, we believe your home should be a haven, a place of relaxation and comfort. But life gets busy, and cleaning chores can often fall by the wayside. That’s where we come in.

Our team isn’t just experienced, but passionate about delivering a personalized touch. We take pride in understanding your unique needs and exceeding expectations.
