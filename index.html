Residential Cleaning

Maintaining a clean and tidy home not only makes it look great, but it also contributes to a healthier living environment. However, keeping a large or small home clean is a time-consuming and effortful task. That's why you can rely on J&N Nunes for outstanding residential home cleaning services. Our team of professionals will take care of the cleaning, so you can spend your valuable time doing the things you love!

Commercial Cleaning

No matter what type of business you have, first impressions are important. That's why it's important to keep your premises clean and tidy. Our expert cleaning team understands that each commercial property is unique and requires a different approach. Our services are designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes. We offer a variety of services, including office cleaning, Janitorial, Stripping & Waxing, Buffing and Scrubbing & Floor Cleaning.

Post Construction

Are you in need of post-construction cleaning services? Look no further than professional cleaning services! J&N Nunes will help remove and dispose of all leftover scrap and unwanted materials from newly constructed or renovated buildings and construction sites. Don't let the mess of post-construction work weigh you down - let the professionals handle it with ease!

Pressure Washing

At J&N Nunes, pressure washing (also known as power washing) is a technique that uses high-pressure water spray to eliminate dirt, mud, grime, dust, loose paint, and mold from various surfaces and objects, such as buildings, vehicles, and concrete surfaces

About us

Back in 2019, our family decided to start a new chapter, leaving Brazil for bigger things in Columbus, Ohio. We jumped into cleaning homes, apartments, and work spots.

Our motto? Responsibility, realness, and full-on dedication. Every house we clean reflects our commitment, our journey, and our wish to make every client feel like their place gets the love our own home does.

At J&N Nunes Cleaning Services, we believe your home should be a haven, a place of relaxation and comfort. But life gets busy, and cleaning chores can often fall by the wayside. That’s where we come in.

Our team isn’t just experienced, but passionate about delivering a personalized touch. We take pride in understanding your unique needs and exceeding expectations.
