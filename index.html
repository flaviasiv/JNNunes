Residential Cleaning

Maintaining a clean and tidy home not only makes it look great, but it also contributes to a healthier living environment. However, keeping a large or small home clean is a time-consuming and effortful task. That's why you can rely on J&N Nunes for outstanding residential home cleaning services. Our team of professionals will take care of the cleaning, so you can spend your valuable time doing the things you love!
Office Cleaning

Office cleaning services are crucial for maintaining a productive, healthy, and professional work environment. Our comprehensive office cleaning services cover every aspect of the workspace to ensure that it is clean, hygienic, and inviting for both employees and clients.
Post Construction

Are you in need of post-construction cleaning services? Look no further than professional cleaning services! J&N Nunes will help remove and dispose of all leftover scrap and unwanted materials from newly constructed or renovated buildings and construction sites. Don't let the mess of post-construction work weigh you down - let the professionals handle it with ease!
Move in & out Cleaning

Moving can be stressful, and the last thing you want to worry about is cleaning up after you're done. J&N Nunes Cleaning is here to help. We provide comprehensive move-in and move-out cleaning services, so you can focus on settling into your new home without the added stress of cleaning up the old one.

About us

In 2019, our family embarked on an exciting new journey, relocating from Brazil to Columbus, Ohio to pursue new opportunities. We established J&N Nunes Cleaning Services, driven by our core values of responsibility, authenticity, and unwavering dedication.

At J&N Nunes, we firmly believe that your home should be a sanctuary, a place of tranquility and comfort. However, we understand that the demands of daily life can make it challenging to keep your space clean and organized. This is where our passionate and experienced team comes in.

We are committed to delivering a personalized touch, tailored to meet your unique needs and exceed your expectations. Each space we clean reflects our dedication to quality and our desire to ensure that every client feels their space is cared for with the same attention as our own home.
