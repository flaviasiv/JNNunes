About us

In 2019, our family embarked on an exciting new journey, relocating from Brazil to Columbus, Ohio to pursue new opportunities. We established J&N Nunes Cleaning Services, driven by our core values of responsibility, authenticity, and unwavering dedication.

At J&N Nunes, we firmly believe that your home should be a sanctuary, a place of tranquility and comfort. However, we understand that the demands of daily life can make it challenging to keep your space clean and organized. This is where our passionate and experienced team comes in.

We are committed to delivering a personalized touch, tailored to meet your unique needs and exceed your expectations. Each space we clean reflects our dedication to quality and our desire to ensure that every client feels their space is cared for with the same attention as our own home.
